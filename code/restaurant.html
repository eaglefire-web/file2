Our Story

Founded in 2010, Bernabas began as a small bistro with a passion for locally-sourced ingredients and artisanal cooking techniques. What started as a humble neighborhood spot has grown into one of the city's most celebrated dining destinations.

Our philosophy is simple: exceptional ingredients treated with respect, prepared with skill, and served with warmth. Every dish tells a story of tradition meeting innovation.
